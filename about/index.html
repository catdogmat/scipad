About Us

Welcome to sciPAD Answers! We are a small team of students who made this site to help others view answers after phones were banned.

Our mission is to provide a free, easy-to-use platform for students to access sciPAD answers and resources.

We are not affiliated with Silverback Academic Media or the official sciPAD books. This is a student-run project.

If you have any questions or feedback, please feel free to contact us.

Wanna help us impove the site? Check out our GitHub repo!
